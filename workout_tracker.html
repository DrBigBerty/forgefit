Garage vs Spare Room Split
Garage phase: All equipment work — bar, bench, kettlebells, dumbbells
Spare room finisher: 8-10 min bodyweight floor work before shower, no equipment needed
Treadmill sessions take place fully in the spare room
Progressive Overload Rules
Every 2 weeks: If all sets/reps completed cleanly, add 2.5kg to bar lifts
Kettlebells: Add 1-2 reps OR move up one KB size
Floor finisher: Add 2-3 reps per exercise every 2 weeks
Treadmill: Every 2 weeks, raise one stage incline by +1 OR speed +0.2 km/h
Rule: If form breaks down, hold weight and add a rep instead
Rest Periods
Compound lifts: 90 seconds between sets
Supersets: 75 seconds between pairs
Isolation / accessory: 60 seconds between sets
Floor finisher: 45 seconds between exercises

How it Works

On this device: Your Master Key is saved in your browser. The Bin ID (your unique data store address) is also saved locally.

On a new device: Open this HTML file, go to Settings, paste your Master Key and Bin ID, hit Save & Sync. Your data loads instantly.

Auto-sync: Every time you tick a set, log a weight, or mark a session complete, your data saves to the cloud automatically.

Privacy: Your data bin is private by default. Only someone with your exact API key and Bin ID can read it.
